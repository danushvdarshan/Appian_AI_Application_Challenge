--------------------------------------------------
Section 1: General Guidelines
--------------------------------------------------
Claims agents must review each case carefully to ensure all regulatory
and internal requirements are met prior to approval.

--------------------------------------------------
Section 1.1: Standard Review Timeline
--------------------------------------------------
All standard claims should be reviewed within five (5) business days
from the date the case enters the Review stage.

--------------------------------------------------
Section 2: Risk-Based Escalation
--------------------------------------------------
Claims involving high-risk categories, regulatory exposure, or incomplete
documentation must be escalated to a senior reviewer.

--------------------------------------------------
Section 3: State-Specific Handling
--------------------------------------------------
Agents must follow additional state-level rules where applicable. State-
specific requirements override general SOP guidance when conflicts arise.

--------------------------------------------------
Section 5: Documentation Standards
--------------------------------------------------
All decisions must be documented in the case record with references to
the applicable policy or regulation.
